Subtraction

Subtraction takes one amount away from another. If you have a things and remove b of them, you have:

a - b = c

One way to picture it is to start at a on a number line and take b steps to the left — this is called counting back. Press play, then replay it: each time it starts at a new random number and takes a different amount away, reading each number aloud as it hops. The dots still standing at the end are the answer.
