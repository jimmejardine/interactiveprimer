Telling the Time

Reading a clock face

A clock has the numbers 1 to 12 spaced evenly around its face. It has two hands that point at those numbers:

  • the short hand shows the hour;
  • the long hand shows the minutes.

Watch the long hand to read the minutes:

  • pointing to 12 means o'clock;
  • pointing to 3 means quarter past;
  • pointing to 6 means half past;
  • pointing to 9 means quarter to.

Each number the long hand passes is 5 minutes — so going all the way round the 12 numbers makes 60 minutes, which is one whole hour.

The rules in one place

  • the short hand shows the hours; the long hand shows the minutes;
  • long hand on 12 = o'clock, 3 = quarter past, 6 = half past, 9 = quarter to;
  • each clock number the long hand passes is 5 minutes;
  • there are 60 minutes in an hour.

See it on a clock

Here the short hand points to the 3 and the long hand points to the 12, so the time is 3 o'clock.
