Ratio and Proportion

Ratio and proportion are how mathematics handles comparison and scaling — mixing paint, reading a map, converting currency, splitting a bill, working out speed, or watching money grow. Master them and a huge slice of everyday maths falls into place.

We start with ratios (comparing parts), move to proportion (how one quantity drives another), and finish with percentages in action — change, working backwards, and compound growth.
