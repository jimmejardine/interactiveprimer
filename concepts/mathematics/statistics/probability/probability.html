Probability

Probability is the mathematics of chance — putting a number between 0 and 1 on how likely something is to happen. From a single coin flip it grows into the tools that power risk, genetics, insurance and machine learning.

This course starts by measuring the chance of one event, then learns to combine events (and / or), and finally to reason with diagrams and with information you already know.
