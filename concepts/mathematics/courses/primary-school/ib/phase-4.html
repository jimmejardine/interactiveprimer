IB PYP Mathematics — Phase 4

The final phase of the PYP continuum (roughly ages 9–12), the bridge into the MYP. Large numbers and all four operations, fractions, decimals, percentages and ratio, the first algebra of patterns and functions, area and volume, coordinate geometry, and data with averages, probability and pie charts. Builds on Phase 3; continues in the Middle Years Programme.
