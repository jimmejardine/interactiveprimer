Canada Secondary School Mathematics

Intermediate and senior mathematics following the pan-Canadian (WNCP) curriculum, organised around the four strands used across the provinces: Number, Patterns & Relations, Shape & Space, and Statistics & Probability. The intermediate years (7–9) build fluency in proportional reasoning, algebra and geometry; the senior years (10–12) branch into functions, trigonometry, calculus and statistical inference.
