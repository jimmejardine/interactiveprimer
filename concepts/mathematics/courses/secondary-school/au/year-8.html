Australia Year 8 Mathematics

Year 8 (ages 13–14) rounds out the lower-secondary core: number sets, roots, index laws and standard form; linear relations, slope and systems of equations; the idea of a function; transformations, congruence, similarity and the Pythagorean theorem; and analysing bivariate data. Builds on Year 7.
