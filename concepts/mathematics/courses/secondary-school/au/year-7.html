Australia Year 7 Mathematics

Year 7 (ages 12–13) is the year of proportional reasoning — unit rates, direct and inverse proportion, percentages and scale — together with full arithmetic on negative numbers and decimals, the first two-step equations and inequalities, the geometry of circles and solids, and an introduction to probability. Builds on Year 6.
