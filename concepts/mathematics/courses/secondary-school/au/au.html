Australia Secondary School Mathematics

Lower- and senior-secondary mathematics following the Australian Curriculum (ACARA), organised by its strands: Number, Algebra, Measurement, Space, Statistics and Probability. Years 7–10 build the core of number, algebra and geometry; the senior years (11–12) branch into functions, calculus, trigonometry and statistical inference.
