South African Grade 12 Mathematics (Matric)

The final year of school (age 17–18), ending in the National Senior Certificate examination — the "matric". Grade 12 completes sequences & series, adds logarithms and inverse functions, the financial mathematics of annuities, and — the capstone of school maths — differential calculus. Greyed items are topics still to be written. Builds on Grade 11.
