Year 1 (Computing)

Year 1 (ages 5–6) is the very start of computing. Children learn that a computer follows instructions — a precise list of steps called an algorithm — and they begin giving those steps themselves, first with a floor robot they can steer around the room and then with colourful blocks in ScratchJr.
