Australia Primary School Computer Science

Primary computer science the way Australian schools teach it, year by year — from Foundation through Year 6. It follows the Australian Curriculum — Digital Technologies: thinking computationally, building and following algorithms, block-based programming, understanding digital systems and networks, representing data, and being a safe, thoughtful digital citizen. Each year is a course you can focus on and work through.
