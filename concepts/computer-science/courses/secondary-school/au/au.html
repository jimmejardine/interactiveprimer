Australia Secondary School Computer Science

Secondary-school computer science following the Australian Curriculum: Digital Technologies (ACARA) across Years 7–10, and the senior secondary computing courses (state offerings such as Digital Solutions, Software Engineering and Computer Science) in Years 11–12. The Lower Secondary years (7–10) build algorithms, programming, data representation and how computers and networks work; the Senior Secondary years (11–12) reach into object-oriented programming, data structures, algorithms, security and the theory of computation.
