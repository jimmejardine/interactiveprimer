Year 9 Computer Science

The final year of Key Stage 3 (age 13–14) rounds out the foundations before GCSE. You handle strings and lists, break programs into reusable subroutines, meet your first named algorithms (searching and sorting), and see how logic gates and truth tables underpin every machine. Networks, cyber security and the wider impacts of technology complete the picture. Work through the topics below in order.
