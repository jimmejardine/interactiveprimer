GCSE Year 11 Computer Science

The final GCSE year (ages 15–16) rounds out the specification: writing programs that are robust and well tested, understanding the systems software that sits between hardware and applications, going deeper into networking protocols, and meeting relational databases and SQL. It closes with cyber security and the legal, ethical and social impacts of computing. Work each group in order.
