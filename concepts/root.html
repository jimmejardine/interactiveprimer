The Tree of Knowledge

Welcome to the Interactive Primer — a single, growing tree of knowledge. Every idea lives on its own page and names the ideas it builds on, so the whole of learning links together into one map you can climb from the very first concept to the most advanced. This page is the root: everything reaches back to here.

Pick a branch to begin:

  • Arithmetic — where everyone starts: counting, adding, and the patterns hidden inside numbers.
  • Calculus — the mathematics of change and motion: slopes of curves, instant speed, and adding up the infinitely small.
  • Welcome, Instructors — if you'd like to build lessons rather than learn from them, start here.
  • The Orphans — a maintenance corner where any not-yet-connected concepts hang until they find their place on the tree.

Use the map above and below to see how concepts connect, and the menu (top right) to switch theme or language.

The Interactive Primer is a work in progress, and we value your feedback early on — it helps us find the best way to teach each concept. Our roadmap is vast; if you'd like to see what's planned, browse the topic roadmaps for Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science.
