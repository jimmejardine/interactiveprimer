Kinds of Energy

Energy is a bit like a costume — it can turn up wearing lots of different outfits. We spot which kind it is by what it does. Here are the ones you meet every day:

  • Light energy — you can see it (a lamp, the Sun).
  • Heat energy — it warms things up (a radiator, a fire).
  • Sound energy — you can hear it (a drum, a speaker).
  • Movement energy — it makes things go (a rolling ball, a running child).
  • Electrical energy — it flows in wires to power our gadgets.

Here's a neat trick that catches lots of people out: one thing often gives out several kinds at once. Switch on a lamp and it turns electrical energy into light — but touch it after a while and you'll feel heat too. One gadget, two kinds of energy pouring out.
