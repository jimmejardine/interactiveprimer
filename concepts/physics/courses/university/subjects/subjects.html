Physics by Subject

Rather than march year by year, you can follow a single subject all the way up — from its first appearance in the foundation year to the specialist final-year course. Fourteen strands run through an undergraduate physics degree; pick one and climb it.

The fourteen subjects

  • Mathematical Methods
  • Classical Mechanics
  • Waves and Optics
  • Electromagnetism
  • Statistical Mechanics & Thermodynamics
  • Special Relativity
  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Condensed Matter Physics
  • Particle Physics
  • Nuclear and Atomic Physics
  • General Relativity
  • Astrophysics and Cosmology
  • Fluid Dynamics
  • Computational Physics
