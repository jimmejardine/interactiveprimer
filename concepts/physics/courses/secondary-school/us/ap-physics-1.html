AP Physics 1

AP Physics 1 is the College Board's first algebra-based physics course — a full year of mechanics: kinematics, Newton's laws, circular motion, energy, momentum and simple harmonic motion. It is a common US equivalent of the first year of A-level. Builds on high-school forces & motion.
