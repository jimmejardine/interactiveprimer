Grade 5 Physical Science (NGSS)

Grade 5 (ages 10–11) sharpens the particle idea of matter, introduces gravity as the pull toward Earth, and looks out at the Sun, Earth and solar system. NGSS 5-PS1 (matter), 5-PS2 (gravity) and 5-ESS1 (stars and the sky). Builds on Grade 4; leads into middle school.
