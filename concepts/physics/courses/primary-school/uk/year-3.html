Year 3 Physics — UK National Curriculum

Year 3 (ages 7–8) is where forces get their name. Children meet forces and magnets — pushes and pulls, how some forces need contact while magnets act at a distance — and explore light and shadows. Alongside, they keep building their working scientifically skills: asking questions and running fair tests.
