United Kingdom Primary School Physics

Physics in the English National Curriculum for primary school, year by year. Primary science isn't split into separate subjects, so these courses gather the physics strands of each year — forces, electricity, light, sound, materials, Earth and space — plus the working scientifically skills that run through them all.

Each year is a course you can focus on and work through.
