Year 5 Physics — UK National Curriculum

Year 5 (ages 9–10) is the great forces year — gravity, air and water resistance, and the clever machines (levers, gears and pulleys) that make a small push do a big job. Children also look up at Earth, the Sun and the Moon. Builds on Year 4.
